About me

Who am I?

Davie Johnson Plastering is run by Davie Johnson. Davie completed his City & Guilds qualifications in the year of 1982, and Davie Johnson Plastering was established in 2001.  Davie believes in transparency, and unlike some trades does not feel the need to elaborate on his experience or skills.  Davie feels that any other job would not give him the reward nor challenge you in the way that he feels plastering does.

After he qualified, Davie spent several years working in many different parts of the country,also working in Germany and on projects such as Euro Disney. Then eventually returning to the North East to build up his own business.  His time with these firms not only hone his skills, but helped to develop the attention to detail that Davie Johnson Plastering is known for today.

 

My Approach

We believe that what the customer wants most are two things - honesty and good workmanship.  Our aim with Davie Johnson Plastering is to provide both on every single job undertaken, week in week out. We are very proud of the excellent client feedback that Davue Johnson Plastering receives, and the reputation that we have built up.

At Davie Johnson Plastering we don't make promises that we can't keep.  We don't offer endless services that we are not skilled in.  We don't book in work and not turn up.  We do what we know and we stick to this. Davie and his team are passionate about their work, and in making sure that the expectations of every single client are exceeded, leaving them happy to recommend us.  The outstanding reviews that Davie Jonson Plastering has from customers speaks volumes.

 

Peace of Mind

We appreciate that employing a tradesman can be daunting. We will create minimum disturbance to you and your home or work place with any work carried out. You can expect us to always be polite and do our utmost not to disturb your usual routine. We make minimum mess, provide extensive plastic sheeting and will always clear up at the end of a job.

Davie Johnson Plastering holds Public Liability Insurance up to £1,000,000 to cover for any accidental damage done to you or your property whilst we are on site.
